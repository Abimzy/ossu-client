Reset progress button

Hello, everyone

Recently there were issues, when students could not update their profiles to the most recent curriculum state.

To solve this problem we decided to add 'Reset Progress' button to the profiles page. This button will delete your current progress and download the latest version of curriculum. Please use this feature with discretion as we won't be able to restore your progress once it has been reset.
